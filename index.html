About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow and Lab Manager in the Bioinformatics and Machine Learning Lab (BML) at the University of Missouri. My research focuses on deep learning for protein structure prediction, protein complex modeling, model accuracy estimation, and computational biology.
